# Brand And Theme Guide

Nuts Pet Food should feel like a real Bali kitchen making honest food for real pets. The site should be simple, authentic, and beautiful before it is clever.

## Core Message

Use this message as the north star:

> Natural canned pet food for dogs and cats, handmade in Bali with locally sourced organic ingredients, ordered easily through WhatsApp.

Every major page section should support one of these ideas:

- real food
- local Bali sourcing
- small-batch care
- no preservatives, no fillers, no artificial shortcuts
- simple WhatsApp ordering

## Voice

Use plain, concrete language.

Good:

- "Handmade in Bali"
- "Real chicken, fish, vegetables, and rice"
- "Order through WhatsApp"
- "No preservatives or fillers"
- "Cooked by our family in Bali"

Avoid:

- generic premium lifestyle copy
- unsupported medical or veterinary claims
- exaggerated guarantees
- dense nutrition jargon
- corporate-sounding phrases like "best-in-class pet wellness solution"

## Visual Direction

The current theme uses a warm food palette:

- warm cream backgrounds
- peach section backgrounds
- orange and gold calls to action
- deep red headings and accents
- dark ink body text

This works for the brand because it feels handmade, edible, sunny, and local. Do not replace it with a cold blue, monochrome black-and-white, luxury beige, or generic tech gradient style unless the brand direction changes.

## Typography

- Body text uses Inter from Google Fonts.
- Brand/display text uses the local Mabook font from `src/fonts/mabook/`.
- Keep display font usage limited to headings, logo-like text, and small brand accents.
- Keep body copy highly readable on mobile.

## Imagery

Prefer images that show the actual product, pets, packaging, ingredients, or Bali production context.

Good image subjects:

- cans, labels, and portions
- pets eating the food
- ingredients before cooking
- founder/kitchen/process details
- delivery or local Bali cues

Avoid generic stock pet images that could belong to any brand. If adding generated or stock images, make sure they do not make the product look different from reality.

## Layout Rules

- Keep the page single-purpose and easy to scan.
- Keep the primary CTA tied to WhatsApp.
- On mobile, the order path should be reachable without hunting.
- Do not hide the product behind a long brand story.
- Use sections in this priority order: hero, why/what, proof, ordering, contact.
- Avoid adding separate pages unless there is a clear SEO or content reason.

## Content Rules

Be careful with claims around:

- "organic"
- "human-grade"
- health benefits
- delivery coverage
- availability
- price

If a claim is operationally sensitive, prefer softer wording or ask the business owner to confirm it.

## Accessibility And Trust

- Keep visible focus styles.
- Use descriptive alt text for real product and pet images.
- Keep emojis supplemental, not the only meaning.
- Use semantic headings in order.
- Make contact information visible in text, not only embedded in buttons.

Trust is more important than visual complexity for this site.
